About Us

3D Modelling of various assets, props and characters ranged from low-poly to high poly based on consumer needs. Texturing is also done.

Think Dimensions. Think Us.

We adapt dynamic work styles, highly productive with an eye for detailing.

Extensive background in the digital/traditional arts including character design , product design , architchural 3D elevation & promise to deliver high quality output with photorealistic shaders & renders.
